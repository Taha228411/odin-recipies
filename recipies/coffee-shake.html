Refresh yerselves with a Coffee Shake!

Description

Thick & delicious tahini coffee date shake made with creamy coconut milk and naturally sweetened with dates. This easy coffee date shake recipe has a hint of rich chocolate flavor from cacao powder and even packs in sneaky veggies! You’ll love this shake for a wonderful breakfast or afternoon pick-me-up.

Ingredients

  • 3 large Medjool dates, pitted
  • ½ cup coconut milk (best if cold!)
  • ½ cup cold brew or leftover cold coffee
  • ¾ cup frozen cauliflower
  • 1 tablespoon tahini
  • 1-2 tablespoons cacao powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

    • Optional add-ins:

  • 1 teaspoon chia seeds

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a high powered blender: pitted dates, coconut milk, cold brew, cauliflower, tahini, 1 tablespoon cacao powder and vanilla extract.
  2. Feel free to add any optional add-ins to your smoothie if you’d like.
  3. Blend until smooth, adding more milk to thin, if necessary.
  4. You can also add another tablespoon of cacao powder if you want more chocolate flavor. Pour into a glass and enjoy! Serves 1.
  5. Feel free to double the recipe to serve 2.
